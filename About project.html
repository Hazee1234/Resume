IOT-Based Suspect Smort Entrance Monitoring System

The virus is airborne and the lack of testing equipment and social distancing has caused a rise in the number of Covid positive cases . Common symptoms of Covid are fever, cough, body ache, breathing difficulty, and a decrease in sense of taste and smell . Cases , where people exhibited no symptoms, were reported too . The Internet of things gives solutions to real-time problems. It is useful to have a COVID-19 prediction system to identify disease by detecting symptoms using IoT based devices. Sensors measure various health parametric values and send them to the cloud. These data can be retrieved in the future to study the situation. The Internet of things deployed here has made this an efficient system by helping to transfer data remotely.
